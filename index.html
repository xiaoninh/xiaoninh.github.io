Senior Capstone Project


NeoGenomics
Laboratories




From September 2017, I started to work on my Senior Capstone Project with four other UCI students for NeoGenomics, a cancer diagnostic company in Orange County. Our team is responsible for implementing a Framework for Doctor Scheduling.


Project in Human-Computer Interaction


Redesign Canvas



During Spring 2017, our team of five students in the HCI project class decided to choose Canvas, a Learning Management System currently using by UCI, as our target for the class project. We conducted usability research to identify usability issues of current Canvas web system, developed design solutions to fix those issues, and finally evaluated the usability of redesign solutions.



Project in the Social and Organizational Impacts of Computing


Ethnographic Research
at UCI eSports Arena



During 2017 Spring, I was a part of a team project analyzing organizational process

and technology-in-use in the gamer community at UCI eSports Arena. Our focuses were on the communication and interactions that take place between gamers during the game, and how the environment and hardware facilities have shaped their interactions.


Observational Research


Case Study of Bilingual Service


at Chinese Baptist Church of Central Orange County



During 2017 Winter, I conducted a case study at Chinese Baptist Church of Central Orange County to study the relationship between the organization and the information system they use. I performed on-site observations and focused on the effects that the bilingual service have brought to church events.
